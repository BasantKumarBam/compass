About Career-Compass

Introduction to Career-Compass

Career-Compass is a student-focused platform designed to help young individuals discover the right career path based on their interests, skills, and goals. We understand that choosing a career can be confusing, especially with so many options and limited guidance. That's why Career-Compass provides personalized career quizzes, expert insights, and carefully selected resources to help you make informed choices.

Our mission is to help you understand who you are, what you are good at, and what careers might be a perfect fit for you.
